Leagues of the BattleTech Universe
  • Step-by-step guide
  • Outline layout

Joining a league will improve your skill and enjoyment with the BattleTech universe. Some of the best 'Mech pilots in the worlds are found on the internet, in rooms that anyone can access and is welcome. This room will help you with software, training and finding a league to join.

Patches and Add-ons

  • The NetMech add-on is required to play any MechWarrior2 series simulator over the internet, LAN and/or modem.
    ICQ allows you to communicate with others on the internet.Very popular and in some cases required to have ICQ loaded on your system and you have an ICQ number.

Game Servers

  • Kali. Most BattleTech simulatory leagues are found on Kali. A lifetime membership costs $20.
    The Internet Zone is free and is a good environment to learn about on-line gaming and ettiquite.
    MPlayer is another free game server that would be a good place to learn about on-line gaming.

On-line Leagues

MechWarrior2:NetMech DOS

  • The Grand Council is still the one of the oldest NetMech leagues on the internet. The GC NetMech Kali server also hosts more users than nearly all other Kali servers. A great place to hone your skills in a kill or be killed environment.
    The Registry is another elder within the NetMech internet leagues.
    The World NetMech League is a new league that is interested in cutting out the red tape associated with turn-based leagues. With a turn every week, this league is fast-paced.
    The Europa Netmech League is a true multi-national league that welocomes pilots from any country. If you are looking for a league that caters to Europe and the world, this league is it.

MechWarrior2:NetMech Win95

  •   Mech3League is one of the only NetMech for Windows95 league on the internet. Constant battles and friendly people.make this league quite fun.
      MPLayer NetMech League is another reare NetMech for Windows95 league. BadKarma.net calls this league "...one of the most colorful".

MechWarrior2 Mercenaries:Mercnet 1.06

  • The Astral Dominion League is an in-depth league. Planets are held and lost, factories increase your firepower and the other units try to take it away.
    The Galactic Battletech League is beginning with all units fighting the Battle of Tukayyid.. The outcome of those series of battles determine the course for the rest of the league.
    The Killing Zone: Mercs is an in-depth stock 'Mech league that adds some twists to the mercs that play.
    The 3055 Mercs BTech League is a league of stock 3055 'Mechs. The BattleTech 'realism' is always on your mind in a stock 'Mech battle.

MechWarrior2 Mercenaries:Mercnet 1.1

  • The Astral Dominion League is considering a Mercnet 1.1 league. Check out the site and speak up if you want it..

*** IMPORTATNT: Before you play on the internet ***

  • Check out BadKarma's League Site. You will find information for both the green to the elite pilots.
  • Report to the simulator training room for more information on leagues and internet play.
